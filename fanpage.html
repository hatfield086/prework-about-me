About Mumford & Sons

  • Members - Marcus Mumford, Winston Marshall, Ben Lovett, Ted Dwane, Rob Moose
  • Established in 2007
  • Genre - Alternative Rock, Folk Rock & Indie Folk
  • Founded Gentlemen of the Road - A record label that promotes bands to visit small towns and villages that normally are not toured. This allows local Business to make money
  • Five albums, "Sigh No More" was their first
  • Songs "I Will Wait" & "Little Lion Man" are their biggest hits
